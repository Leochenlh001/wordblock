Privacy Policy

WordBlock Chrome Extension

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Home Support

1. What We Process

WordBlock processes data only to provide vocabulary lookup features triggered by user actions.

  • Local browser storage: lookup cache, history, favorites, and AI settings.
  • Network requests: selected word content sent to dictionary/etymology services when lookup runs.
  • If AI is enabled by user: word and related context may be sent to user-configured AI provider.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Provide definition, etymology, morpheme analysis, AI explanation, history, and favorites.
  • Improve lookup continuity through local caching and local learning records.
  • Not used for advertising personalization.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • When AI is enabled, requests are sent to the provider configured by the user.
  • Third-party provider handling follows that provider's own policy.

4. Retention & Control

  • Data is stored locally in Chrome storage by default.
  • Users can disable AI any time.
  • Users can remove data by clearing extension storage or uninstalling the extension.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the Support page.
